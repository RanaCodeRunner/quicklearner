WHILE LOOP

~You can use the WHILE loop to repeat a sequence of statements until the controlling condition is TRUE. The condition is evaluated at the start of each iteration. The loop terminates when the condition is FALSE. If the condition is FALSE at the start of the loop, then no further iterations are performed.

Syntax:-

WHILE conditon LOOP
statement1;
statement2;
. . .
END LOOP;

~If the variables involved in the conditions do not change during the body of the loop, then the condition remains TRUE and the loop does not terminate.

Example:-

declare
      A number;
      B number;
      C number;
Begin
      B:=1;
      C:=1;
      A:=5;
      WHILE(C<=A)
      LOOP
            B:=B*C;
            C:=C+1;
      end loop;
          dbms_output.put_line('FACTORIAL OF '||A|| ' IS: '||B);
end;
